**energy mood tracking visualization**

an R shiny app that helps visualizes my csv file with energy level, and colored by mood

Energy scales 1-10. Mood described by three axis: 

|Symbol | Axis Name | The "Feeling" | Mapping to Traditional Moods | 
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
|O | Open | Outward flow, connection, "yes" energy.| Cheerful, Social, Curious |
|P | Push | High-pressure friction, heat, "no" energy.| Angry, Frustrated, Assertive |
|S | Sink | Heavy, inward gravity, "still" energy.| Sad, Reflective, Tired |

The csv file is manually extended each day. It contains the columns of:

| Date | Energy | O | P | S |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
